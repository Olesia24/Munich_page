5 reasons to visit the most beautiful city

Munich!

1. Nationaltheater München

historic opera house where you can enjoy outstanding performances

2. Englischer Garten

A huge park with picturesque alleys, lakes and traditional Bavarian beer halls

3. Schloss Nymphenburg

A luxurious palace complex with beautiful gardens and an art collection.

4. Pinakotheken

A complex of art museums with a rich collection of European paintings.

5. Hofbräuhaus

A renowned beer garden with a rich history, serving traditional Bavarian food and beer.
